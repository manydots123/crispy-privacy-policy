Privacy Policy – Crispy

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

Crispy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. No Account Required

Crispy does not require users to sign up or log in. We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or track any personal information or usage analytics. All data remains on your device unless explicitly shared by you.

The only data sent externally is the text you input when generating a recipe. This input is securely sent to Google's Gemini API to create your recipe. However:

  • The request is anonymous
  • No personal identifiers are attached
  • We do not know who sent what prompt

3. On-Device Storage

Any saved recipes or user preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not store any data in the cloud or external databases.

4. Third-Party Services

Crispy uses the Gemini API by Google for AI-powered recipe generation. While your prompt is sent to their service to generate content, no personal data is included, and the prompt is not linked to your identity.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or cloud databases.

5. Children’s Privacy

Crispy is intended for general use and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

6. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored, and all preferences are saved locally, we minimize any risks to your privacy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top and make the new policy available in the app or via our website (if applicable).
